About Me

My introduction

Welcome! My name is Nicolle, and I am a passionate and experienced artist specializing in tattooing, permanent makeup, and body piercing. With over 4 years of experience in the industry, I have dedicated my career to helping clients express their individuality and enhance their confidence through art. Fluent in English, Russian, and Romanian, I strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for all. Whether you’re looking for a custom tattoo, flawless permanent makeup, or expert piercing services, I’m here to bring your vision to life. Let’s create something unforgettable together!
